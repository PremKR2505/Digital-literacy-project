DOs--
-Do think twice about what you share on social media. Remember "once on the internet, always on the internet."Be cautious of your digital footprint
-Do click only on links you are familiar with and trust.
-Do secure accounts with 'two-factor authentication’.
-Do form as many connections as you can with other students, professionals, or people related to your studies or field of interest.
-Do keep your and others privacy in mind
DONTs--
-Don't spend too much time on social media
-Don't bully others or encourage bullying on the internet
-Don't trust everything you see on internet some might be trolls or even bots
-Don't post illegal content on social media
-Don't post in the heat of the moment, think before you post something
