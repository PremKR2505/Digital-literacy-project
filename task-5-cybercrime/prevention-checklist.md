'Stay Safe Online' checklist
-Enable Two Factor Authentication
-Don't use public WiFi for sensitive task
-Encrypt your data with tools like a trusted VPN
-Do not approve random payment requests on your UPI app
-Do not share your personal details carelessly on the internet
-Do not share OTPs carelessly
-Report Cybercrime immediately if you or someone you know are scammed, immediately call the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 or report the incident at the official National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
-Practice safe online shopping: only buy from sites you trust and buy products taht have secure payment options
